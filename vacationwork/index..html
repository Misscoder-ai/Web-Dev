What is cybersecurtiy?

Cybersecurity refers to the practice of protecting systems, networks, and programs from digital attacks. These cyberattacks usually aim to access, change, or destroy sensitive information, interrupt normal business processes, or extort money from users. Good cybersecurity measures help ensure the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of data.

Types of Cyber Threats

Cyber threats come in many forms, such as malware, phishing, ransomware, and social engineering attacks. Each type targets different vulnerabilities, and understanding them is the first step to defending your systems. Learning about these threats helps individuals and organizations stay vigilant and proactive in their security strategies.

Importance of Strong Passwords

Strong passwords are a critical layer of defense in cybersecurity. A weak password can easily be guessed or cracked, giving attackers unauthorized access to your accounts and data. Using long, complex, and unique passwords for each account significantly reduces the risk of breaches and keeps your information safer.
